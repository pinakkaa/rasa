RASA – Baked
Salmon

Tender, flaky, and fragrant. A RASA-spiced fillet finished with lemon, ginger-garlic, and a drizzle of golden garlic-coriander butter — elegant enough for a dinner party, simple enough for any night.

Prep Time 10 min
Cook Time 30 min
Serves 2–4 people
Difficulty Simple

The Method

5 Simple Steps

1
Prepare the fish

Wash the salmon thoroughly to remove any odour. Pat dry, then make a few shallow slits across the fillet to allow the spices to penetrate deeper.

2
Massage in RASA

Rub RASA powder generously all over the fillet — no additional salt needed. Work it into the slits for maximum flavour.

500 g fish
2 tbsp RASA powder
300 g fish
1 tbsp RASA powder
3
Add paste & lemon

Spread ½ tbsp ginger–garlic paste over the fillet, then squeeze the juice of 1 fresh lemon over the top. Let it rest for a few minutes to absorb.

4
Top with lemon slices

Arrange lemon slices over the fillet before cooking. If you're pan-frying instead of baking, set the lemon slices aside and add them at the very end.

5
Bake or fry

Choose your preferred cooking method below.

🔥 Bake
180°C for 30 minutes until the flesh flakes easily.
🍳 Pan-Fry
Low heat, lid on — 15 mins skin-side, 10 mins other side. Flip once more and add lemon slices to finish.
From the Kitchen

Chef's Tip

👨‍🍳
Pro Tip

"A quick garlic-coriander drizzle turns a simple baked fish into something truly special."

Sauté chopped garlic and coriander in 1 tbsp olive oil or butter over medium heat for just 2 minutes until fragrant and golden. Pour this directly over the cooked salmon just before serving. Pair with a fresh side salad for a light, complete meal.
