RASA – Smoked Chicken
Ghee Roast

A coastal Mangalorean classic reborn. Deeply smoky, richly buttered, and finished with the bold warmth of the RASA blend — bold enough to impress, simple enough for any night.

Prep Time 10 min
Cook Time 40–55 min
Serves 4–6 people
Difficulty Simple
RASA Blend
Two's a Date
Smoked Chicken Ghee Roast

Main Ingredients

+

The Method

5 Simple Steps

1
Marinate the chicken

Wash chicken pieces and place in a bowl. Add 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste and mix well to coat evenly. Set aside while you prepare the rest.

2
Add RASA & salt

Add RASA Smoked Chicken Ghee Roast powder and salt to the marinated chicken. Mix well until every piece is thoroughly coated.

1 kg chicken
3.5 tbsp RASA + 1 tsp salt
500 g chicken
2 tbsp RASA + ½ tsp salt
3
Sear in oil

Heat 1 tbsp oil in a frying pan over low heat. Add the RASA-coated chicken pieces to the pan.

4
Cover & simmer until tender

Add the RASA-coated chicken, cover and cook on simmer until tender, turning pieces once to evenly distribute cooking.

Note:  When you open the pan and the base feels dry or is catching, add 3 tbsp water and cover to continue cooking.
⏱ 500g — approx. 40 mins ⏱ 1 kg — approx. 55 mins
5
Finish with ghee & jaggery

When the chicken is nearly done, add 2 tbsp ghee and 1 tbsp powdered jaggery. Increase the heat to medium–high and fry for 5 minutes.

Finish by adding curry leaves for an extra layer of aroma. Serve hot.

From the Kitchen

Chef's Tip

👨‍🍳
Pro Tip

"The secret is in the final fry — ghee, jaggery, and high heat for that signature caramelised crust."

Once the chicken is cooked through, fry it in ghee and 1 tbsp powdered jaggery for just 5 minutes on medium–high heat. This brings out the rich, dark, glossy coating that defines a true Ghee Roast. Add curry leaves at this stage for an unmistakable aroma that fills the kitchen.
